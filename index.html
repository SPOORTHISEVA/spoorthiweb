About SPOORTHI SEVA SAMSTHA

Spoorthi Seva Samstha is a registered society with its office located at Dondapadu Village, Thulluru Mandal, Guntur Dist., A.P., INDIA. Our core mission is to undertake relief and rehabilitation for victims of natural and man-made calamities such as cyclones and famine.

We are dedicated to promoting welfare programs for women, children, youth, and the aged, fostering health and family welfare awareness, providing health facilities in urban and rural areas, and taking measures for AIDS prevention. Our efforts also include protecting the environment and natural resources.

Furthermore, we strive to enhance the economic development of the poorer sections of society, promote unity, discipline, service, and patriotism, and organize holiday home camps and cultural tours for children. We are committed to transparent and accountable operations, ensuring every effort contributes to building a more equitable and compassionate world.
